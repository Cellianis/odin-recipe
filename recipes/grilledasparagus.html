Grilled Asparagus

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Fresh Asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the gate
  2. Lightly coat the asparagus spears with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook Asparagus on the preheated grill, turning often until lightly charred and fork-tender about 3-5 minutes.

Tips

Pair this with a grilled chicken marinade recipe or grilled salmon.
