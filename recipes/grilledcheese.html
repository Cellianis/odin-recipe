Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese is easy to make and perfect for a quick lunch. Munch on this cheesy recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of preferred bread, honey wheat suggested
  • 1 slice American Cheese
  • butter, salted

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
  2. Take your butter and spread it on one side of each of your two slices of bread.
  3. Place 1 slice of bread, butter down, on your preheated skillet.
  4. Place your slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of the bread in the skillet
  5. Place the second slice of bread, butter side up, on top of your cheese in the skillet.
  6. Cook until golden brown on both sides. Flip about once a minute to check both sides and ensure an even golden brown exterior has been achieved
  7. Once done, set on a plate and let cool 1 minute before eating.

Tips

You can add more cheese if this isn't cheesy enough for you or switch up the types of cheese and bread you use to see what you like. There's almost never a wrong version of this!
